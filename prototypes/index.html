cycle-in — choices-page prototypes

Static design mockups, same demo data in both. Dev-server only — not part of the production build. Both render in light and dark (they follow your OS theme). Decision (Ryan, 2026-07-10): direction B, completely — kept here for the record; the real UI in src/ui/ is built from B.

A — Practice ledger

One calm list is the hero: a daily practice sheet with serif item names, quiet verbs, and a per-item cadence ring showing how far through its period each item is. Attention chips up top; Planning as a footer section. Light-first.

 B — Balance board

Category-first: a balance bar shows today's attention split against an even-split target, then per-category panels each carry their own day/week/month minutes and due items. Pinned and timed items float in a strip; Planning is a panel. Dark-first, denser, tool-like.

Things to react to: list-first (A) vs category-first (B) organization · the cadence-ring vs balance-bar signature · where Planning lives · verb placement (quiet row under an opened item in A, inline per-panel in B) · overall temperature (paper vs instrument panel).
